School Fees- 2020

The fee of R 1,000 – Annual administration fee is paid every new year. Below is the 2020 fees structure:



Half-Day

R 3 000

Full Day

R 3 400

Full Day Nursery

R 2 800




Fees are paid monthly in advance by the 1st of every month.

Included in the school fees is a sports programme, all children receive a ½ hour coached sports lesson once a week according to their ability. Parents receive a written report from the coaches twice a year.

Manners 4 Minors program is also included in the school fee, this is a ½ hour interactive puppet show aimed at introducing children to the benefits of having good manners and displaying acceptable social skills.
Parents are communicated what is taught to them each week so that these can be enforced at home.

Meals: All meals and snacks are provided by the school. A bi-weekly menu is available on Umbilically. Special dietary requirements are considered.
